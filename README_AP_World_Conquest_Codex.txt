AP World: Conquest Codex Quiz RPG

How to play:
1. Open ap_world_conquest_codex_quiz_rpg.html in any browser.
2. Use the Campaign Map for the original conquest quiz battles.
3. Open Explore and choose an AP World unit from the unit selector.
4. Move with WASD, arrow keys, or the on-screen controls.
5. Step near a unit-based boss and press Enter or Attack Nearby.
6. Correct answers damage the boss sprite. Wrong answers cost explorer HP and add terms to your Revenge List.
7. Use the Codex Library to search all embedded terms and definitions.
8. Progress saves automatically in your browser with localStorage.

No internet or external libraries are needed.
